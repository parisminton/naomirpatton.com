Home

I hail from the suburbs of Maryland outside Washington, D.C., but I go where the opportunity and the experience take me.

I've worked as a journalist and communications professional for nearly 20 years. There is little I haven't done at weekly newspapers, medium and large dailies; in public affairs work, as well as communications and publishing -- and a few things in between. I now look forward to expanding my communication and storytelling platforms with digital media.

Resume

Newspapers

Staff Writer, April 2003 - present.
Detroit Free Press | Detroit, MI

Provide Detroit city government and general assignment coverage. Also covered Detroit Police and the Wayne County Sheriff's Office, and provided western Wayne County and southeast Michigan coverage; including local government, education, business and features.

Staff Writer, December 1999 - March 2003.
Wisconsin State Journal | Madison, WI

Provided general assignment coverage; including police, city and county government in Madison and Dane County.

Staff Writer, September 1993 - December 1993.
The Onion | Madison, WI

Reviewed films and music for satirical weekly.

Staff Writer/Managing Editor, October 1992 - August 1993.
Milwaukee Community Journal | Milwaukee, WI

Provided city and state government, education and features coverage for African-American weekly newspaper. Assigned stories, and managed production and editorial content for the WEEKEND edition.

Staff Writer/Editor, November 1990 - May 1992.
The Daily Cardinal | Madison, WI

Covered city, state, national and international news for student newspaper; including arts and sports. Edited "Page 2" essay/opinion column.

Communications | Publishing

Editorial Assistant, August 2002 - March 2003.
Wisconsin Historical Society Press | Madison, WI

Researched, copy edited, and proofread book projects and in-house newsletter.

Editorial Assistant, January 1999 - January 2000.
Journal of Human Resources | Madison, WI

Maintained peer-review of manuscripts for quarterly, academic economic journal.

Communications Coordinator, August 1996 - January 1999.
Household & Institutional Products Information Council/Consumer Specialty Products Association | Washington, D.C.

Wrote and edited briefs/media responses, and analysis of state and U.S. Environmental Protection Agency/Superfund hazardous waste regulations. Assisted strategic development and implementation of media campaigns with contract public relations firms; produced one-sheets, press releases, collateral materials. Tracked media coverage/placement.

Assistant to the Director of Publications, September 1994 - August 1995.
Optical Society of America | Washington, D.C.

Produced project presentations and budget reports for publications department, which published a dozen academic journals for the international professional association. Edited website content. Managed copyright requests and registered copyrights with the Library of Congress.

Editorial Intern, September 1991 - December 1991.
Wisconsin Alumni Association | Madison, WI

Wrote articles, copy edited and assisted production for WAA magazine, On Wisconsin, and alumni newsletter.

Wrote liner notes for MTV's 1993 "Arrested Development Unplugged" CD.

Non-media

Resident manager, November 1999-June 2001.
UW-Extension J.F. Friedrick Inn and Conference Center | Madison, WI

Live-in manager and night auditor; provided guest services.

Shelter advocate, September 1999-September 2000.
Domestic Abuse Intervention Services (DAIS) | Madison, WI

Facilitated resident intake and provide client services at a shelter for abused women and families serving Dane County, Wis. Answered 24-hour Crisis Line, provided non-emergency safety planning and protection/restraining orders instruction, and provided referrals to community resources and various DAIS programs.

Education

Attended the University of Wisconsin - Madison; majored in history.
